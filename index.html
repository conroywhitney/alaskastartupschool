Alaska Startup School

Alaska Startup School is an 8-week program based on the Lean Launchpad cirriculum professionally developed by world-renown startup experts. In the spirit of Lean Startup, we will use Customer Development techniques to iterate on your Business Model Canvas and achieve Product/Market Fit.

You will spend ~20 hours/week between May 6th, 2018 and June 30th, 2018 watching online video lectures from the "How to Build a Startup" Udacity course; participating in weekly group calls; communicating with your peers via Slack; working closely with your team; and (most importantly) interviewing 10-15 different customers each week.

Space is limited. Applications are due by April 29th, 2018 at 11:59pm. Applicants will be notified on April 30th, 2018.

Questions? Contact us at: alaskastartupschool@gmail.com
